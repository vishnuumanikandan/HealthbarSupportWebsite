Frequently Asked Questions

To log a meal, tap the + button on the home screen or navigate to the Meals tab and tap Log a Meal. You can search for a food item by name, scan a barcode using your camera, or enter custom nutrition values manually. Once you've added all items in your meal, tap Save. Your XP and calorie totals will update immediately. You can log meals throughout the day — breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks are all tracked separately.
Every healthy action you take earns you XP (experience points). Logging meals, hitting your calorie goal, staying within your macros, maintaining your streak, and completing daily challenges all reward XP. As you accumulate XP, you level up and advance through ranks — starting at Initiate and progressing through ranks like Warrior, Champion, and beyond. Each rank unlocks new profile badges, challenges, and cosmetic rewards. Your current rank and XP progress are always visible on your profile screen.
The Purity Score is a daily quality metric that reflects how clean and balanced your eating was on a given day. It's calculated based on factors like your macro balance, how close you stayed to your calorie goal, the ratio of whole foods to processed items in your log, sugar and sodium intake, and whether you hit key micronutrient targets. A score of 100 represents a perfect day by your profile's standards. The Purity Score is tracked over time so you can spot trends and improve week over week — think of it as your daily nutrition grade.
During onboarding, HealthBar collects your age, biological sex, height, weight, and activity level, then uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR). This is multiplied by your activity factor to estimate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). From there, your calorie goal is adjusted based on your stated objective — whether that's losing weight, maintaining, or building muscle. If you feel your goal needs adjusting, you can update your health profile at any time through the Profile → Settings → Update Health Profile flow.
Yes. To edit a logged meal, go to the Meals tab and tap on the meal entry you want to change. From there you can adjust portion sizes, swap out individual food items, or add new items to the meal. To delete a meal entirely, swipe left on the meal entry and tap Delete, or open the meal and tap the trash icon in the top-right corner. Note that editing or deleting a meal will recalculate your daily totals, Purity Score, and any XP awarded for that day.
A streak requires at least one meal to be logged before midnight in your local timezone. If you miss a full day without logging anything, your streak will reset to zero the following morning. However, HealthBar offers one Streak Shield per week — a grace item that automatically activates the first time you miss a day, preserving your streak. You can earn additional Streak Shields by completing weekly challenges or reaching milestone levels. You'll always receive a push notification in the evening if you haven't logged anything yet that day.
You can update your health profile at any time by going to Profile → Settings → Health Profile. From there you can edit your weight, height, age, activity level, and goal. Changes take effect immediately and will recalculate your daily calorie and macro targets. If your stats have changed significantly — such as after substantial weight loss or a change in training volume — it's a good idea to refresh your profile so your goals stay accurate and meaningful.
Yes, core functionality works offline. You can log meals, view your history, and track your progress without an internet connection. Any data entered while offline is stored locally on your device and automatically synced to the cloud the next time you come online. Barcode scanning and food database searches require a connection, but previously searched or frequently used foods are cached locally for offline access. Push notifications and leaderboard features require connectivity to function.
Your privacy is a top priority. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using TLS. Your personal health data — including weight, meals, and profile details — is never sold to third parties and is not used for advertising. You can export or permanently delete your account data at any time from Settings → Account → Manage Data. HealthBar is fully compliant with Apple's App Store privacy guidelines and applicable data protection regulations. For full details, please review our Privacy Policy linked in the app.
